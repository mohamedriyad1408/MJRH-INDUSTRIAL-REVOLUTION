# MJRH V4 — The 6 Enterprise Layers
## The Structural Anatomy of a Business Operating System

### Layer 1: The Constitutional Layer
- **Role:** Sovereignty and Authority.
- **Ownership:** Legal entities, Organization structure, Mandates.
- **Constraint:** Everything below must have a sovereign root here.

### Layer 2: The Logic & Policy Layer
- **Role:** Governance and Strategy.
- **Ownership:** Rules (Policies), Success criteria (OKRs), Business models.
- **Constraint:** Determines what is "Legal" or "Valid" for the layers below.

### Layer 3: The Capability & Readiness Layer
- **Role:** Provisioning and Potential.
- **Ownership:** Competencies, Capacity, Resource pools (People, Assets, Cash).
- **Constraint:** Work cannot start without confirmed readiness here.

### Layer 4: The Runtime Execution Layer
- **Role:** The Pulse of Work.
- **Ownership:** Value streams, Activities, Real-time state transitions.
- **Constraint:** Produces the continuous stream of system events.

### Layer 5: The Evidence & Integrity Layer
- **Role:** Memory and Trust.
- **Ownership:** Immutable Ledgers, Artifacts, Audit trails, Proof of work.
- **Constraint:** The absolute Source of Truth for the past.

### Layer 6: The Insight & Evolution Layer
- **Role:** Learning and Adaptation.
- **Ownership:** Performance analytics, Capability mutation, System re-config.
- **Constraint:** Drives the "Self-Correction" loop of the entire institution.
