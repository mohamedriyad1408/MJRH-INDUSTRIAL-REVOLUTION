# MJRH V4 — The Enterprise Constitution

## I. Dimension: Existence & Identity
- **Purpose:** Defining "Why" and "Who". Legal and sovereign boundaries.

## II. Dimension: Authority & Stewardship
- **Purpose:** Mapping "Rights to Decide" and accountability chains.

## III. Dimension: Intent & Strategy
- **Purpose:** Directives, roadmap, and success criteria (OKRs).

## IV. Dimension: Value Logic
- **Purpose:** Metabolism of the business. How inputs transform to valuable outputs.

## V. Dimension: Pulse & Operations
- **Purpose:** Repeatable execution of value streams (The Core Rhythm).

## VI. Dimension: Capacity & Provisioning
- **Purpose:** Supplying Human, Financial, Material, and Knowledge assets.

## VII. Dimension: Vigilance & Control
- **Purpose:** Sensory feedback. Comparing Reality with Intent and Policy.

## VIII. Dimension: Memory & Wisdom
- **Purpose:** Cumulative record of every event. Institutional learning.

## IX. Dimension: Evolution & Mutation
- **Purpose:** The ability to self-reorganize and adopt new business capabilities.
